02 /Legislation Stack

Three stacked laws, / one rolling deadline.

Not a single bill — a layered framework that's been building since 2020. The market opportunity is the wave of mandatory upgrades it has set in motion.

JULY 1, 2020
SB 712 — Clean Waterways Act
Foundation. Forced BMAP local governments to write OSTDS remediation plans. Transferred FL's septic program from DOH → DEP.
JULY 1, 2023
BMAP ENR Mandate Active
New septic systems on lots ≤1 acre in BMAP impacted areas must be ENR-OSTDS (65% nitrogen reduction).
2023
HB 1379 — IRL Protection Program
Created the Indian River Lagoon Protection Program. Since Jan 1, 2024, ALL new septic in IRL counties (any lot size) must be ENR-OSTDS.
2026 SESSION
HB 1417 — Amendment
Pushed the IRL existing-system deadline from 2030 to 2035, capped at properties ≤10 acres. Mandatory permit notification on every repair/modify/replace permit before the deadline.
JULY 1, 2035
IRL Compliance Deadline
Every existing septic system in Volusia, Brevard, Indian River, and St. Lucie counties (≤10 acres) must connect to sewer if available, or upgrade to a 65%+ nitrogen-reduction system.
